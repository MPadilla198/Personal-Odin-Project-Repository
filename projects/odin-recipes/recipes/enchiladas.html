Chicken Enchilada Recipe

Description

A delicious recipe for homemade chicken enchiladas! Try these to get a taste of a staple Mexican dish.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Chopped onion
  • Shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Dried oregano
  • Ground black pepper
  • Can tomato sauce
  • Chopped green bell pepper
  • Minced clove garlic
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt (Optional)
  • Water (Optional)
  • Flour tortillas
  • Jar taco sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all inredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Heat oil in a nonstick skiller over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink and juices run clear, 5 to 7 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to a cutting board and slice into cubes. Return chicken to the skillet.
  3. Add onion, Cheddar cheese, sour cream, parsleym oregano, and black pepper. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese melts. Stir in tomato sauce, green pepper, garlic, chili powder, and salt. Add water, if needed.
  4. Spoon chicken mixture into tortillas and roll into enchiladas. Arrange enchiladas, seam-side down, in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Top with taco sauce and remaining Cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheat oven, uncovered, until cheese has melted, about 20 minutes. Let cool briefly before serving.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
