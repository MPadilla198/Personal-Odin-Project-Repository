World's Best Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is the recipe to make the world's best lasagna! It has won multiple awards and critics all agree that this is the best lasagna recipe in existence.

Not only is this recipe delicious but it is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Minced Onion
  • Crushed Garlic Cloves
  • Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • Cans Tomato Sauce
  • Cans Tomato Paste
  • Water
  • White Sugar
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg
  • Sliced Mozzarella Cheese
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all your inrgredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch over over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, Italian Seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occaisionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. To assemble, spread meat sause in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat suace, overlapping slightly. Spread the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon more meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray and make sure the foil does not touch cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasasgna for 15 minutes before serving.
